About AYUSH
The Ministry of Ayush was established on November 9, 2014, to revive and promote ancient systems of medicine like Ayurveda, Yoga, Unani, Siddha, and Homoeopathy. Initially, the Department of Indian System of Medicine and Homoeopathy (ISM&H), formed in 1995, was in charge of these systems. It was renamed the Department of Ayush in November 2003 to focus more on education and research in these fields.
